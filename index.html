There are lots of vegetarians out there who are missing out on the taste of a truly great steak. What if we could make artificial meat that tasted just like that tasty New York strip but without the cow? Scientists are working on it!

Enter Belly Button Biodiversity (B3), Inc. The scientists at B3 are working around the clock to isolate the flavor of beef from bacteria. The source of the bacteria? The belly button of course! Be among the volunteers to join the B3 family. All it takes is a quick swab and some background information. Once registered, volunteers can use the B3 dashboard to see how their samples compare with other volunteers from around the world. If your sample becomes the key to a truly great "feak" (pronounced like fake), you will receive royalties for life. Who knew that the key to your retirement may be right there in your belly button!
